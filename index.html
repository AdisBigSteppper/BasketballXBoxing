Dangerous influence of boxing on health

According to CNN research

Each year 13 boxers die in the ring

A long term boxing career can expose one to repeated blows to the head, which can result in what's known as “Punch Drunk Syndrome,”

which is characterized as a condition where the sufferer often slurs their speech or may not be as stable when standing or moving, typical behaviors observed in people who may be intoxicated.

Main example of a boxer who dealt with such disease's - Muhammad Ali. Who struggled with the symptoms of parkinson his whole life after ending his sport career.
